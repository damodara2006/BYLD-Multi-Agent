# Nifty 50 Hits All-Time High Led by Banking Heavyweights

A sudden rush of Foreign Institutional Investor (FII) buying propelled the Nifty 50 index to an unprecedented all-time high today. The surge was largely driven by leading private banks beating market earnings expectations.
