# Bharti Airtel ARPU Hits New High Following Tariff Hikes

Benefiting from recent premiumization shifts and entry-level tariff hikes, Bharti Airtel reported an industry-leading Average Revenue Per User (ARPU). Telecom analysts predict further steady growth in the next two quarters.
